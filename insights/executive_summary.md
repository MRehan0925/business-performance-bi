# Executive Summary

## Overview
This analysis evaluates paid business performance by integrating revenue, engagement,
and training data into a single analytics framework. The objective was to identify
early churn signals, measure training effectiveness, and provide leadership with
actionable insights.

## Key Outcomes
- Approximately **25% of paid businesses show declining engagement trends**
- Businesses that **complete trainings demonstrate higher activity levels**
- Engagement decline typically appears **one month before complete inactivity**
- Training completion is a leading indicator of sustained engagement

## Business Impact
- Enabled proactive intervention for at-risk businesses
- Reduced reliance on reactive churn analysis
- Provided leadership with clear performance visibility
- Supported data-driven prioritization for success teams

## Strategic Recommendation
Focus on:
- Early outreach to businesses showing activity decline
- Improving training completion rates
- Monitoring month-over-month engagement trends as churn signals
