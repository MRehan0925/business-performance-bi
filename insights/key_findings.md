## 2. Churn Risk Indicators
- A consistent decline in appointments is a strong churn predictor
- Businesses with multiple declining months show higher inactivity probability
- Early-stage decline offers the highest opportunity for intervention

## 3. Training Effectiveness
- Training completion correlates with improved engagement
- Businesses that miss scheduled trainings show higher inactivity rates
- Training impact is most visible within the first month post-completion

## 4. Operational Insights
- Manual reporting delays risk detection
- Centralized metrics significantly improve response time
- Clear KPI definitions align teams on performance evaluation
